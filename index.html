About Me

I am a Puerto-Rican, born and raised in New York City. I have 2 children. Justin Adam (21 yrs old) and Emmyline Amyah (15 yrs old). I served in the United States Navy Submarine Force for 6 years and have traveled to Spain, France, Greece, Italy and Turkey. During that time I gained and learned an array of various software, programs and databases. I am in the process of acquiring HTML, CSS & Javascript skills to prepare for a career in web development i.e. “Front-End Development” through “The Knowledge House”. One short term goal is to be accepted into a Web Development “BootCamp” school with the recommendation through “The Knowledge House” to further my education and learn “Back-End Development” on my way to becoming a “Full-Stack Developer”. I am looking to meet, connect, network and develop relations in the Web Development Tech Industry with “Tech Startups”, “Entrepreneurs”, “Business Owners”, “Professionals”, “Free-Lancers”, “Policy-Makers”, “Game-Changers”, and “Heroes”.
